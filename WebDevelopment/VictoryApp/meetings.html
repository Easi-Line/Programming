Meetings Schedules

Fellowship Meetings

Join us every Thursday for worship, teaching, and fellowship.
Our fellowship meetings are a time to come together as a family, lift our voices in praise, and receive inspiring messages from our leaders.
We encourage everyone to participate, share testimonies, and build lasting relationships in a warm and welcoming environment.

Cell Meetings

Small group meetings for deeper connection and growth.
Cell meetings provide a more intimate setting where members can discuss the Word, pray for one another, and support each other personally.
These gatherings foster accountability, spiritual growth, and genuine friendships that extend beyond the weekly meetings.

Bible Study

Weekly Bible study sessions to grow in the Word.
Our Bible studies are interactive and open to all, regardless of your level of understanding.
We explore scripture together, ask questions, and seek practical ways to apply biblical principles to our daily lives.

Community Outreach

Spreading love and support through outreach programs.
We are committed to making a positive impact in our community by organizing charity events, visiting the less privileged, and sharing the love of Christ.
Everyone is welcome to join us as we serve, give back, and make a difference together.
